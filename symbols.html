olive tree

olive tree

- the olive tree symbolizes peaceful resistance
- average lifespan of an olive tree is
300-600 years; represents Palestinian
rootedness in their land
- resilience & endurance

key of return

key of return

- represents the "right to return"
- many Palestinians kept the keys to their
homes when they were displaced from their homes in Nakba
- the keys are passed down generations as
a reminder of their old homes and symbolizes
their right to return

watermelon

watermelon

- after the 6-day war in 1967, the Israeli
government banned displays of the Palestinian flag
- Palestinians began using the watermelon
as it represents the flag colors, however
those colors were banned from being used as well
- the ban was lifted in 1993
- now used to avoid censorship and
shadowbanning on social media
- symbolizes Palestinian solidarity
